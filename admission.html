Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must meet academic qualifications as per the selected course requirements.

Required Documents

Submit academic transcripts, ID proof, passport-size photos, and entrance exam scores if applicable.

Application Mode

Students can apply online through the registration portal or visit campus for offline admission.

Steps to Apply

  1. Fill out the online registration form.
  2. Upload required documents.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Receive confirmation from the admission office.
