About Parul University

Our Vision

To provide quality education, foster innovation, and develop socially responsible professionals equipped for global challenges.

Rules & Regulations

  • Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory for all students.
  • Students must carry their ID cards at all times inside campus.
  • Strict discipline must be maintained in classrooms and hostels.
  • Use of unfair means during examinations will result in strict action.
  • Ragging is strictly prohibited and punishable as per university norms.
  • Respect towards faculty, staff, and fellow students is compulsory.

Code of Conduct

Students are expected to maintain academic integrity, uphold ethical behavior, and contribute positively to the university environment.

Any violation of university policies may lead to disciplinary action.
